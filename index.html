Jack's Package Store was founded in 1984 as one of Mountain Home, Arkansas's first liquor stores, and has since become a local institution. For over 30 years, we have focused on providing quality customer service, having a wide range of quality products and putting the needs of our customers first. Our tenets have not changed, but recently we have been working towards an creating an ever improving retail experience as well as lowering our prices to be increasingly competitive. If you haven't made it in in a while, we highly encourage you to stop by and see what's improved; we think you'll be pleasantly surprised! Hope to see you soon.
